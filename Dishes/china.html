Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds halibut, cut into 4 pieces
  • 3 green onions, cut into 3 inch lengths
  • 2 fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 6 leaves napa cabbage, sliced into 4 inch pieces
  • 2 slices fresh ginger root, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ¼ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ⅛ cup water
  • Whole Fish (Sea Bass, Tilapia, or Snapper): 1 fish (about 1-1.5 pounds)
  • Vegetable Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Scallions (Green Onions): 3-4, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Cilantro: A small bunch (optional), for garnish
  • White Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
  • fresh cilantro sprigs, for garnish
  • crushed red pepper flakes to taste

Procedure

Preparation Steps

Prepare and clean the fish:

1. Clean the Fish:
 Remove any scales with a fish scaler or the back of a knife,Pat the fish dry with paper towels.
2.Score the Fish:
Using a sharp knife, make 2-3 diagonal cuts on each side of the fish. These cuts should be about 1/2 inch deep. This helps the fish cook evenly and allows the flavors to penetrate.

prepare the steamer:

3. Set Up the Steamer:
 Fill a wok or large pot with enough water to steam the fish without touching it.
4. Prepare the Steaming Plate:
Place a few scallion pieces on a heatproof plate to act as a bed for the fish. This prevents the fish from sticking to the plate.
5.Add Onions:
 Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
6. Add Green Chilies and Ginger-Garlic Paste:
Add the slit green chilies and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell of ginger and garlic goes away.
7. Add Tomatoes:
Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
8. season the Salad:
Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the salad,Optionally, drizzle a balsamic glaze for a touch of sweetness,Sprinkle salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
9. Add Boiled Chickpeas:
Add the boiled chickpeas to the masala. Mix well to coat the chickpeas with the masala.
10.Add Water:
Add a cup of water (or as needed) to achieve the desired consistency. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. If using amchur powder and/or kasuri methi, add them at this stage.
11. Add Garam Masala:

Sprinkle garam masala over the curry and mix well.
12.Adjust Seasoning:
Taste and adjust salt and other seasonings if needed.

Garnishing and Serving:

13.Garnish:
Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro.
14.Serve:
Serve the salad fresh at room temperature to enjoy the full flavor of the ingredients.

Enjoy your homemade Steamed Fish!
