Black Bean Soup

Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 medium celery stalk, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 4 cups (2 cans) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt (optional, for serving)
  • Tortilla chips (optional, for serving)

Procedure

Preparation Steps

1.Heat the Oil:
In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
2.Sauté Vegetables:
Add the diced onion, garlic, carrot, celery, red bell pepper, and jalapeño (if using). Sauté for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
3. Add Spices:
 Stir in the cumin, chili powder, and smoked paprika. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the spices are fragrant.
4. Add Beans and Broth:
Add the black beans, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and bay leaf. Bring the mixture to a boil.
5.Simmer:
 Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6.Blend the Soup:
Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency. You can blend all of it for a smooth soup or blend just half to keep some texture.
7. Season and Serve:
Remove the bay leaf. Stir in the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt and tortilla chips if desired.
