Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, minced
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

Preparation Steps

Preparing the Gezpacho:

1. Prepare Tomatoes: :
 Core and roughly chop the tomatoes. Place them in a large mixing bowl.

2.Prepare Cucumber:
Peel the cucumber, roughly chop it, and add it to the bowl with the tomatoes.

3.Prepare Bell Pepper:

Seed and roughly chop the red bell pepper. Add it to the bowl with the other vegetables.

4. Prepare Onion and Garlic:


Peel and roughly chop the red onion. Peel the garlic cloves. Add both to the bowl with the other ingredients.
5.Blend Vegetables:
Transfer the chopped vegetables to a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. You may need to do this in batches depending on the size of your blender.
6.Combine Blended Mixture:
Pour the blended vegetable mixture into a large bowl or pitcher. Stir to combine.
7.Add Liquids and Seasonings:
Stir in the tomato juice, olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, and ground cumin (if using) into the blended mixture. Mix well to ensure all ingredients are combined.

8.Chill the Soup:
Cover the bowl or pitcher with plastic wrap or a lid. Refrigerate the gazpacho for at least 2 hours, or until thoroughly chilled. This allows the flavors to meld toget
9.Adjust Seasoning:
After chilling, taste the gazpacho and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more salt, pepper, or vinegar to taste.
10.Serve and Garnish:
Stir the gazpacho well before serving. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh herbs like basil or parsley.

Enjoy your homemade Gezpacho!
