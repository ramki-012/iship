Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cups dry lentils
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • ½ cup spinach, rinsed and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Procedure

Preparation Steps

Preparing the Lentil Soup:

1. Rinse Lentils : Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.

2.Sauté Vegetables:

a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables begin to soften, about 5-7 minutes.

3.Add Garlic and Spices:

Add the minced garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and smoked paprika to the pot. Stir and cook for another minute until the spices are fragrant.

4. Add Lentils and Tomatoes:

Add the rinsed lentils, diced tomatoes (with their juices), and bay leaf to the pot. Stir to combine..

5.Add Broth:

 Pour in the vegetable broth or water. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer. Cover and cook for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.. 6. Season: Remove the bay leaf. Season the soup with salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste..
7. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro. Enjoy!.

Enjoy your homemade Lentil Soup!
